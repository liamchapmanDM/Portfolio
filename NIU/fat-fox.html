The Fat Fox

- Mobile App

Android Mobile App for a sports-bar based in Southsea. HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PhoneGap. Watch the demo of the developed prototype below.

This project was completed during my final year of University, as group work with one other group member. I took the role of developing the application, having supported the design process also. The app was developed using HTML, CSS and JavaScript for the interactive features and paying features. The client was very happy with the app, and we have spoken about further development so that it is ready to introduce into the android app store for users. The app was put through the development framework Adobe PhoneGap in order to be compatible for android devices. This unit was graded first class through feedback from the University of Portsmouth.

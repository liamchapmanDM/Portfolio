Genghis Khan

- Infographic

Infographic based on Genghis Khan. Adobe Illustrator.Click here to view infographic in browser.

This infographic was created using Adobe Illustrator, and involved a range of graphic design techniques in its design. This included setting grids in order to define a layout for the image, kerning and making text appealing. The images and patterns were slowly developed and some come from manipulated images and from classic Mongolian culture. The imagery used in the infographic was further improved upon and advanced during the creation of the motion graphic video which was created afterwards, and can be viewed here.

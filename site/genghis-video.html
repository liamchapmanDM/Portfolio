Genghis Khan

- motion graphic

Motion Graphic based on Genghis Khan. Adobe AfterEffects, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop.

I created this motion graphic based on Genghis Khan for one of my modules, where I was able to use and develop my skills in some different software. This video was created using Adobe After Effects, where I learned some key skills independently, by researching and following online tutorials. I further developed my Adobe illustrator skills also by manipulating images of Genghis to create a coherent video using its imagery. I feel that adding these skills to my arsenal means I can offer more than just web development to clients and employers, as I can create a diverse range of digital content to use on social media channels and websites alike. This motion graphic was also supported in another piece of work in my portfolio which you can find as an infographic based on Genghis.

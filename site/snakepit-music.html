Snakepit Music

- Hybrid Mobile Application

Hybrid Android Mobile Application. HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PhoneGap.

> <

Snakepit Music was a project I completely individually during my second year of university. This was my first experience in developing a mobile app, and I put a focus on designing an easy to navigate application with a professional design and aesthetic. The app was developed using HTML and CSS, with a thorough design process involved, including market research and several prototype designs. The app is responsive for different phone and tablet sizes for the user and was fully functional. Scroll through the above carousel to view how the app looks and works.
